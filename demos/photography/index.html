About me

Hi, I'm Elowen

For over a decade I've been photographing love stories across the country and beyond. I'm drawn to quiet glances, belly laughs, and the small details that make your day yours.

My approach is calm and unobtrusive — I'm there to document, not to direct. You'll barely notice me, but you'll feel it in every frame. Recognised by the National Wedding Awards, I'd be honoured to tell your story too.

— Elowen
